Hello! I'm Yumi, a budding frontend developer based in Brazil. My coding journey involves SheCodes and self-guided exploration. With over a decade in Communications, I specialised in designing user-focused events and providing Visual Facilitation services. In 2017, I shifted gears, dedicating years to planting organic food in agroforestry systems, emphasising the profound link between diversity and abundance.

Since 2023, I've been immersed in frontend development, seeking to blend creativity with technology. The transition from cultivating the land to crafting digital landscapes has been a continuous learning journey. My previous experiences instilled empathy, resilience, adaptability and an appreciation for diversity.

Now, navigating the world of code, I am committed to creating digital experiences centered around inclusivity and responsiveness. Driven by a passion for ongoing learning and innovation, I thrive in dynamic environments where challenges lead to personal and professional growth.

If you are seeking a dedicated, skilled, and multilingual developer who is willing to contribute to your team's success, I would be thrilled to collaborate with you. Let's connect and cultivate something incredible together!
